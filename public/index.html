What is Tour de Pancake?

Riding bikes with friends around Davis County and eating free pancakes. The routes range from easy to difficult. Cyclist and volunteers are welcome to sign up. Volunteers can choose from the following jobs: driver, cook, bringing a side dish, or babysitting. Started by John Evans, a guy who loves riding his road bike and cooking breakfast for people. His wife recently had an accident at home resulting in her becoming a parapalegic. The community came together to help them out, so they would like a way to give back. All procedes from the swag are donated to people at the such-n-such-place who have recently become wheelchair bound.
